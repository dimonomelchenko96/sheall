Payment Methods

Currently, SHEALL accepts payments with Visa and Mastercard.

To make a payment, you will need to provide your banking card details. Your banking and payment information remains confidential and secure in accordance to all relevant privacy and security protocols. Payment details will be sent directly to our corresponding bank's authorisation server via a protected channel (SLL 3.0 Protocol). Data you provide is encrypted and saved only on a specialised payment system server. The Website administrators do not have access to this data.
