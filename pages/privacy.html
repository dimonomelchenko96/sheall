Privacy Policy

Last updated October 2, 2021

Information we collect may include, but is not limited to:

  • Your full name, email address, and phone number;
  • Your preferences in regards to our Services;

We may collect your personal information for business purposes, including to:

When we collect and process personal information, and while we retain this information, we will protect it within all adequate means to prevent loss, theft, unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, modification, and/or any use inconsistent with the terms of this policy or any other Terms and Agreements governing our Company and usage of your information.
