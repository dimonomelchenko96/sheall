Return & Refund Policy
This Return & Refund Policy describes the instances when we may issue a return of funds to you, as well as the refund procedure of our Company.
When we may issue refunds

Instances when we issue a return of funds include:

  • If an expert you have booked a consultation with is unable to meet during the appointed date and time, or otherwise unable to provide you with our Services.
  • If a force majeure occurs, resulting in the inability of either or both parties to meet during the appointed date and time.
  • If our Website experiences accidental or maintenance-related downtime, resulting in your or your advisor's inability to meet during the appointed date and time.
  • If you have a legitimate reason (such as a valid medical emergency).
How we issue refunds

Please be advised that refund request processing may take up to 48 hours.

Upon processing your request for a return of funds, we will correspond as to the optimal refund method, which will typically be issuing a return to the banking account or cardyou have used when paying for our Services.
