FAQ

We have put together the most frequently asked questions by our users. Connect with us if you still have questions, we're happy to chat and help you.

What does SHEALL do?

We offer business consultations for ambitious women. Regardless of the stage your business is in, we are ready to optimise it's strategy, brand identity, growth trajectory, as well as all operational and management processes.

Why is SHEALL only for women?

Our vision is to create a space where women have access to every resource and tool without hitting the glass ceiling so prevalent across industries. This is a safe space to develop your ideas, learn from women professional and entrepreneurs, as well as be a part of a community which champions mutual support, integrity, and the individual prosperity of all.

I'm a company. It is possible to share consultation materials with other employees?

Yes, while only one person should have access to using the SHEALL account, you may, with prior informing of SHEALL and your advisor, invite your colleagues and/or employees to sit in during consultations.

Do you accept international clients?

Absolutely, SHEALL aims to include women from around the world and all walks of life. However, please be advised that our specialists' expertise may be limited to the countries in which they are licensed or otherwise thoroughly aware of their jurisdiction and business practices.

What do I need to start working with SHEALL?

You don't necessarily need to have a business, an idea is enough to start creating a strategy that works for you. In order to attend the consultations, you will need a device with connection to the Internet.

Are these consultations safe/legit?

Absolutely, SHEALL and all of its employees are contractually bound to maintain the security and confidentiality of your personal and business information to the best of our ability. We do not sell or otherwise disclose your information to third-parties.
Our Services are provided by licensed specialists in their respective sector of business. Therefore, to the best of their and our capabilities, the material provided to you during the consultations is maximally accurate, relevant, and useful to your specified business goals.

How do I get started with SHEALL?

Complete our contact request form or book a consultation if you are ready. One of our managers will get in touch with you within 48 hours.
